About Us

Who We Are

AfriSafe Risk Consultants Limited (ARC) was founded in June 2016 as a premier Insurance and Risk consulting firm. Our management team comprises individuals who have worked with some of the top global companies in insurance broking, underwriting and risk consulting. We consolidate the skills, expertise and experiences of some of the top insurance practitioners in Uganda to take brokerage to the next level. With our blend of local and international experiences, we are dedicated to understanding the unique needs and circumstances of each of our client’s before tailoring insurance and risk solutions benchmarked against the very best in the world.

 We are licensed by the Uganda Insurance Regulatory Authority as an insurance brokerage firm and are a member of the Insurance Institute of Uganda (IIU) and the Uganda Association of Insurance Brokers (UAIB).
